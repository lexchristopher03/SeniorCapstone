My Project

Summit Grip introduces a transferable hand-warming system that wraps around your ski pole grip, designed to keep skiers comfortable without the waste of disposable hand warmers. The product will be rechargeable, weather-resistant, and sustainably designed to reduce single-use waste while improving performance and enjoyment on the slopes.

Key Features

Transferable Wrap

Wraps onto common ski pole grips so the system can move between poles without waste.

Rechargeable Heat

Designed for repeat use with a rechargeable power source to replace disposable warmers.

Outdoor Ready

Weather-resistant build targeted for cold, snow, and moisture exposure on the mountain.

Problem

Cold hands reduce comfort and control while skiing. Disposable hand warmers add ongoing cost and create single-use waste.

Solution

Summit Grip is a reusable heating wrap designed to deliver warmth directly to the grip area while staying portable and low-waste.
